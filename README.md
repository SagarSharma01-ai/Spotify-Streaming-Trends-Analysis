## ðŸ’¡ Key Findings

### 1. The "Mobile-First" Reality
I am a mobile-exclusive listener.
- **93.1%** of my total streaming (12,724 tracks) happens on **Android**.
- Only **~7%** occurs on Desktop/Web.

### 2. The Rock Dominance
Unlike generic pop trends, my history is dominated by Classic and Modern Rock:
- **#1 Artist:** **The Beatles** (13.6K Streams).
- **#2 Artist:** **The Killers** (6.9K Streams).

### 3. The "Desktop vs. Mobile" Discrepancy
A deep dive revealed platform-specific favorites:
- **Desktop Favorite:** The song *"Caution"* by The Killers ranks #4 globally but disappears from the Android Top 5, indicating it is a "Work Mode" anthem.
- **Mobile Favorite:** *"Concerning Hobbits"* appears only in the Android Top 5, suggesting it is used specifically for relaxation/sleep away from the desk.
